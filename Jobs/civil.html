Job For Disabled People

  • CIVIL SERVICES

    Educational Qualification: Matriculation, or 10th with 12th class passed candidates or Graduates. Age Limit: The age of the candidate must be in between 21 to 33 years old. PWD Category: Visual impairment, speech and hearing disability, orthopaedic disability, multiple disabilities. A total number of attempts: Candidates belonging to PH or PWD category are eligible to have ten attempts. There are more than 20 Civil services available for PWD category candidates.
