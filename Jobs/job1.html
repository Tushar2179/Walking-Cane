COMMUNICATIVE SPEECH READING

  • Accounts/ Finance Executive

    Must be proficient with QuickBooks. Handles accounts receivable and accounts payable information under the directions of the Finance Manager. Prepares general ledger entries by maintaining records and files. Strong accounts profile- Indian accounting-TDS, Service Tax, GST, PF, ESIC, PT Challans etc. Prepare and maintain Balance Sheet Schedules and Account Reconciliations. Process Inter-company transactions. Have a good working knowledge of the payroll process, including experience with online Third-party payroll processing. Responsible for compiling amounts owed by vendors and suppliers. Proficient with Microsoft Word and Excel. Access experience would be a plus.
