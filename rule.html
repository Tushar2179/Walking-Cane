How To Apply

Step By Step Process on How To Apply Online For Government Jobs in India

Know your skills and background so that you can easily filter out areas where you can find a govt. job.

Make use of a search engine for finding government jobs in India and look for websites that are frequent in updating Govt. jobs.

Find the "application" link at the bottom or within the recruitment post you are reading for which you want to apply and visit that link.

Look in the post if experience is necessary, and apply only if you satisfy the requirement otherwise your application will be a waste of time as it will not get shortlisted.

Keep a copy of the application with yourself for future reference.
